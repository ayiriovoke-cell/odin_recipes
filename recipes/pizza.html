Pizza

Description

Pizza is an oven-baked flatbread topped with tomato sauce and cheese, and usually meats or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3cups warm water (100° F to 110° F)
  • 1 Tbsp granulated sugar
  • 2.25tsp (1 packet) active dry or instant yeast
  • 3.5 to 4 cups all-purpose flour (or bread flour)
  • 2 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup store-bought or homemade pizza sauce (crushed tomatoes with a pinch of salt, oregano, and garlic)
  • 1.5 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Preferred toppings: Pepperoni, sliced bell peppers, onions, or fresh basil
  • Cornmeal or extra flour (for dusting)

Steps

  1. Activate the yeast: In a large bowl or stand mixer, whisk together the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy
  2. Make the dough: Add the flour, olive oil, and salt. Mix on medium speed or knead by hand for 5-7 minutes until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.
  3. First rise: Lightly oil a large bowl, place your dough inside, turning to coat. Cover with a damp cloth or plastic wrap and let it rise in a warm spot for 1 to 1.5 hours, or until it doubles in size.
  4. Preheat and stretch: Preheat your oven to 475° F (246° C). If using a pizza stone, let it heat up for 15-20 minutes. Punch down the dough and gently stretch it into a 12-inch circle on a lightly floured surface.
  5. Assemble: Transfer your stretched dough to a dusted pizza pan or baking sheet. Brush the edges with olive oil, spread an even layer of sauce, and sprinkle with mozzarella and your desired toppings.
  6. Bake: Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.

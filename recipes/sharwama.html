Sharwama

Description

Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern street food consisting of thinly sliced, marinated meat stacked in a cone shape and slow-roasted on a vertical rotisserie.

Ingredients

  • Chicken: 500g boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • Spices: 2 tsp ground cumin, 2 tsp ground coriander, 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp paprika, 1/2 tsp cinnamon, and 2 tsp salt
  • Marinade base: 3 tbsp olive oil, juice of 1 lemon, and 3 minced garlic cloves
  • Sauce: 1/2 cup plain yogurt, 2 tbsp tahini (optional), 1 minced garlic clove, and a squeeze of lemon juice
  • Toppings: Pickled cucumbers, sliced tomatoes, and lettuce

Steps

  1. Marinate: Mix the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, cumin, coriander, turmeric, paprika, and cinnamon together in a bowl. Add the chicken thighs and coat evenly. Leave to marinate for 30 minutes or overnight in the fridge.
  2. Cook: Heat a cast-iron skillet or heavy-based pan over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken for 4-5 minutes on each side until slightly charred and cooked through. Let it rest, then slice.
  3. Assemble: Whisk the yogurt, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic together. Spread the sauce over a warm pita or lavash flatbread, add the sliced chicken, pickles, and tomatoes. Roll tightly and toast the edges on a hot grill.
